Bhotekoshi Whitewater Rafting

A beautiful mountain river only a few hours from Kathmandu offering a relaxed day rafting or kayaking day trips at all classes of challenge. Translating to the river from Tibet, Bhotekoshi is the main branch of the Sunkoshi and it was first descended by a kayak from near the Tibetan border at Kodari. Bhotekoshi is the ideal introductory trip to whitewater in Nepal. A pleasant half day trip, a blue river ,few gentle rapids and glistening white beaches.

Trishuli Whitewater Rafting

The Trishuli is Nepals most popular rafting river. Exciting rapids, good beaches, some easier sections and easily accessible from Kathmandu and Pokhara. Rafting trip on the Trishuli needs no recommendation, thousands of rafters make way into its waters every week. For many, its the ideal trip, just the right length, duration, exhilarating rapids but not challenging for a first timer..

Karnali Expedition - 10 days

Set is the less explored Western Nepal. The Karnali River has some of the biggest and best rapids in Nepal. Couple this in with an amazing journey to the river through the remote and friendly villages around Surkhet, make this a great trip. Youll spend your days running fantastic whitewater, exploring hidden waterfalls and villages and your evenings (and layover day) relaxing on expansive sandy beaches in the midst of jungle clad hillsides. On the lower reaches we often see some spectacular birdlife and wildlife

Tamur Expedition - 12 days

A Magnificent whitewater expedition, one of the best in the World. The river flows along the beautiful and untouched valleys of the Eastern Nepal. Combined with few days of a splendor trek of the Eastern Himalayan outline, this is one of the most scenic river routes offered by Nepal. Draining from the snows of Kanchenjunga, the third highest peak in the world, Tamur is the sixth largest river in Nepal and finds its way from constrained yet magnificent green valleys w which gives rise to 130rapids in just 120 km
