Embark on the ultimate whitewater adventure with the Tamur River Expedition. Trek through the untouched Himalayan trails and conquer over 100 rapids in 120 kilometers of pure adrenaline. ‍A breathtaking journey blending mountain trekking, wild rivers, and raw natural beauty—only in Eastern Nepal.
