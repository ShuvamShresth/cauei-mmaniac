About Us

At UR Nepal, we go beyond just providing you a rafting or kayaking holiday, we give to you an adventure packed full of moments you will never forget, an incredible journey on some of the best white water in the world, showcasing the best of Nepal’s rural landscapes and culture.

Beyond that we also focus on the environment and sustainable tourism, our river trips are ran with the upmost respect for our fragile environment. Not only that but we have a solid commitment to our local community as well. we played a big hand in building the the Bansagu Community School near Bhotekoshi

Ultimate Rivers’ roots are grounded in our deep love for the wild rivers running through Nepal. Operating in Nepal for the last 25 years, Ultimate Rivers, as our name suggests, is the ultimate rafting and paddling company in Nepal when it comes to safety, value for money, quality, service and fun on Nepal’s wild and beautiful white water.
